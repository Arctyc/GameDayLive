# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-15

## Overview

GameDayLive (the app) is designed to provide real-time sports match updates to Reddit communities. We value your privacy and believe in data minimization.

## Information We Collect

- Personal Information: The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information about Redditors.

- Configuration Data: The app only stores configuration settings necessary for its operation, such as the designated subreddit team preferences. These are stored within the Reddit Devvit environment.

- Interaction Data: The app only processes public Reddit content (game threads), and public API data to perform its function. It does not collect user data in any way.


## Third-Party Services

-  The app does not share any data anywhere outside of the posts it makes on reddit.

- The app fetches public sports statistics from the following sources:
    - nhle.com (National Hockey League Enterprises)
