# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-15

## Overview

GameDayLive (the app) is designed to provide real-time sports match updates to Reddit communities. We value your privacy and believe in data minimization.

## Information We Collect

Configuration Data: The app only stores configuration settings necessary for its operation, such as the designated subreddit team preferences. These are stored within the Reddit Devvit environment.

Interaction Data: The app only processes public Reddit content (game threads), and public API data to perform its function. It does not collect user data in any way.

### Information We Don't Collect

The app does **not** collect, process, or store any user information, including:

- Personal information about Reddit users
- User data of any kind, including usernames, comments, or interactions
- IP addresses or device information
- Browsing history or user behavior
- Any identifying information about individuals


## Third-Party Services

The app does not share any data outside of Reddit. While the operator may access application logs for maintenance and debugging purposes, these logs do not contain any user information, in accordance with the information collection policy outlined above.

The app fetches public sports statistics from the following sources:

- nhle.com (National Hockey League Enterprises)

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
